About Me

Hi! I'm Michelle Asuamah a web developer based in Toronto. I first got into coding while searching for a hobby during the 2020 Covid-19 pandemic. What started off as building a simple WordPress blog for fun, turned into a full-blown interest in coding, web development, and design. I soon started self-teaching with available online resources. Eventually I made the commitment to attend coding boot camp to really hone my skills, and transition into a career in tech. I am a highly self-motivated individual who is always eager to build, learn, and create. My technological skills include Javascript, HTML, and CSS.
