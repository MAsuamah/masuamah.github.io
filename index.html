About Me

Michelle Asuamah is an aspiring web developer based in Toronto. She is currently enrolled in boot camp at the University of Toronto. She first got into coding while searching for a hobby during the 2020 Covid-19 pandemic. What started off as building a simple word press blog for fun, turned into a full-blown interest in coding, web development, and design. She soon started self teaching with available online resources and eventually made the commitment to attend coding boot camp to really hone her skills, and transition into a career in tech.
